Each card shows the design at 200px (BotFather upload preview), 100px (profile circle), and 50px (chat-list size). Click Download PNG on the one you like — upload it to BotFather with /setuserpic.

All variations use the Telegram official blue (#3390EC) and dark theme (#17212B). Each PNG is rendered at 512×512, the size BotFather wants. The "Save SVG" buttons give you the vector source in case you want to tweak it in Figma/Illustrator first.
